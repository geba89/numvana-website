Game Modes

ZEN

Endless & Peaceful

  • No time limit, no game over
  • Grid expands when full (37 → 61 → 91 cells)
  • Optional score display
  • Auto-saves your progress
CHALLENGE

Rising Tide

  • Water rises every few swipes
  • Merge 128+ to push water back
  • 50% water coverage = game over
  • Three difficulties: Gentle, Flow, Torrent
DAILY

One Puzzle a Day

  • Same puzzle for all players
  • Seeded tile sequence
  • Star rating: Bronze, Silver, Gold
  • Two attempts per day
